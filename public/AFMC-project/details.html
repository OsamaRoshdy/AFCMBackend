“Medical Mycology in Focus” The First Mycology day program Thursday 05 Sep 2019

It was held on 9th of JAN 2018 under patronage of Maj.Gen.Med.Ayman Shafei director of the AFCM, directed by Prof. Maha Hamdy head of medical microbiology and immunology department, AFCM and organized by staff members of Medical Microbiology and Immunology Department, AFCM to celebrate the distinguished event of “Mycology Week” celebration by CDC in collaboration with WHO; thus, being the first scientific seminar and workshop held by an Egyptian University in this remarkable occasion.

We were honored by Dr. Mostafa Yehia the Dermatology Consultant and the Founder and Head of Mycology Unit, Al-Haud Elmarsud Hospital. He enriched the audience with his fungal infection cases presentation. A “hands on” work shop was held on the same day in Microbiology laboratory, illustrating the conventional methods used to diagnose fungal skin infections. The event aimed at encouraging healthcare providers to “THINK FUNGUS” when symptoms of infection are not cleared off with treatment, since it is a new era of microbial virtue that eventually gain more armory and became more virulent and resistant to known antifungal drugs.
